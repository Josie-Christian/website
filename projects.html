Thistlesticks - Project Timeline

❮ ❯
Duration

This project began in the fall of 2020, as me any my roomate concieved the roleplaying games and mechanics. Over the course of my writing undergrad degree, I refined this project. It is now currently undergoing playtesting and art asset creation and nearing commercial availability

Overview

Thistlesticks is a grimdark roleplaying games about sentient critters surviving under the backdrop of human war, focusing on the setting of the maginot line during the outbreak of world war 2. It features a grid based inventory combat system and unique grain magic system.

The 16th Furl - Project Timeline

❮ ❯
Duration

This project began in the spring of 2023 and is currently ongoing, with the first few chapters available soon!

Overview

The 16th Furl is a hypertextual fantasy epic constructed of disjointed, fictionalized entries, that explores themes of imperialism, fascism, work culture and transness in a world of flying sky islands, airships and most notably- lots of gnomes.
